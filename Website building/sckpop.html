Kpop

Kpop, short for Korean-Pop, is a music genre that originated in South Korea in the 1990s. Kpop consists mainly of Boy and Girl Groups that sing, dance, and rap. Most Kpop songs are heavily inspired by Western hip-hop, R&B, and Rock. Some famous Kpop bands include BLACKPINK and BTS.
