Rap

Rap, or hip-hop, is a music genre originating from Bronx, New York during the early 1970s. Rap is just one of many techniques used in hip-hop, but people define it as its own music genre. Rap became mainstream during the mid 1980s and mid 1990s which is known as rap's "golden age." Some prominent artists in rap today include Kendrick Lamar, A$AP Rocky, and JCole
